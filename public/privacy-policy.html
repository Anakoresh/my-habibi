PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 04.09.2025

At Habibi Hostel, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, booking services, and event/tour request forms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Booking details (dates, number of guests, preferences)

This information is collected when you:

  • Make a booking with us
  • Submit a form for tours, day trips, or events
  • Contact us directly via the website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for:

  • Managing your booking
  • Confirming and organizing tours, trips, or events
  • Sending you booking confirmations and important updates
  • Responding to your inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff members of Habibi Hostel. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

4. Third-Party Services

In some cases, we may use trusted third-party platforms (such as booking websites or payment processors) to manage reservations. These providers have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data (unless we are required by law to keep it)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: habibihostelsrilanka@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
